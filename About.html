About Us

Uniglobe Secondary School, established in 2014 in Kamaladi, Kathmandu, is a NEB-approved private +2 institution offering Science, Management, and Law programs with modern specializations and a student-focused approach. The campus features AC classrooms, a 4,000+ volume library, computer labs, sports facilities, a cafeteria, and a 300+ seat conference hall. Active student clubs and co-curricular events like debates, theatre, and cultural excursions enrich campus life. Admission is based on entrance tests, interviews, and SEE results, with scholarships offered on a merit-cum-need basis. We are committed to creating a better educational experience. Through collaboration, innovation, and listening to your voice, we work continuously to improve and grow.
